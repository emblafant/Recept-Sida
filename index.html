Kladdkaka

Fun Facts

  • i've been following this recipe for 10 years
  • it's amodified recipe of one i found on the internet
  • it's just extra cacao powder and no salt lol
  • i can make this by heart

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2.5 dl sugar
  • 5 tablespoons cacao
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar
  • 1 dl flour
  • 100g butter

instructions

  1. pre-heat the oven to 175
  2. mix the eggs and sugar
  3. add all the dry ingredients and mix it together
  4. add the milk
  5. melt the butter and add it to the mix
  6. butter and bread a baking tray and add the mixture
  7. put it in the oven for 20+ min until desired textured is achived
  8. let it cool

sweet & sour pulled beans

Fun Facts

  • this is really easy to make
  • i almost always have the ingredients at home to make this

ingredients

  • Pulled beans
  • rice
  • eggs
  • sweet & sour sauce
  • roasted onion
  • veggies (optional)
  • vegetable oil

instructions

  1. boil the rice
  2. heat up a pan and pour in the oil
  3. fry the eggs and cut them up in the pan
  4. add the pulled beans to the pan and fry until done
  5. add the sweet & sour sause to the pan and mix
  6. remove the pan from the stove
  7. plate and garnish with rosted onion

Pasta Pesto

Fun Facts

  • i came up with this recipe myself
  • there are no units of messurements for the ingredients, you have to go on feeling
  • this dish is the best comfort food
  • it tastes even better as leftovers

ingredients

  • pasta penne
  • cooking cream
  • cherry tomatoes
  • sundried tomatoes
  • pesto (rennet-free)
  • salt
  • black pepper

instructions

  1. boil the pasta
  2. chop up the tomatoes
  3. when the pasta is done pour out almost all the water
  4. add the cream, pesto and tomatoes
  5. stir until the cream is no longer runny
  6. add salt and pepper
